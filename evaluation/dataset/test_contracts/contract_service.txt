PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is dated January 10, 2026, by and between BuildDraft Partners ("Consultant") and Apex Holdings ("Client").

1. Scope of Work. Consultant shall perform the consulting services described in Statement of Work (SOW) 1.

2. Payment and Invoicing. Client shall pay Consultant at the rate of $150 per hour. Invoices shall be submitted monthly and are due within thirty (30) days of receipt.

3. Confidentiality. Both parties agree to keep all technical and business information disclosed during the SOW strictly confidential during the term and for three (3) years thereafter.

4. Termination. Either party may terminate this Agreement only for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice and failure to cure.

5. Limitation of Liability. The maximum aggregate liability of either party under this Agreement shall be limited to the total fees paid to Consultant under the SOW.

6. Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California, and disputes shall be resolved in San Francisco, California.
